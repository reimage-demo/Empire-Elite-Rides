Accessibility Statement

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Our Commitment

Empire Elite Rides aims to make this website usable by as many people as possible, including visitors who use keyboards, screen readers, browser zoom, voice input, or other assistive technologies.

Accessibility Measures

  • Semantic headings, labels, landmarks, and descriptive alternative text.
  • Keyboard-accessible navigation, forms, buttons, links, and expandable FAQ content.
  • Visible focus styles, responsive layouts, readable contrast, and reduced-motion support.
  • Form status messages announced to assistive technologies.

Need Assistance?

If a page or booking control is difficult to use, call 860-326-1089. We can provide information and help take a reservation request by telephone.

Feedback

Please report the page, control, device, browser, or assistive technology involved. We will make reasonable efforts to investigate and improve the experience.

Third-Party Content

Some supporting services, including externally delivered fonts or browser modules, are controlled by third parties. We welcome reports about barriers encountered while using those components.
