Common questions

Albany chauffeur service, clearly explained.

What areas does Empire Elite Rides serve?

We serve Albany, Troy, Schenectady, Cohoes, and Watervliet, New York. Long-distance and interstate transportation can also be requested through our booking form.

What vehicle is used for chauffeur service?

Every reservation is served in our black 2017 Ford Expedition Limited, offering spacious passenger seating and room for luggage.

Do you provide Albany airport transportation?

Yes. Scheduled airport pickups and drop-offs are available, including transportation to and from Albany International Airport.

How do I request a reservation?

Use our online booking form or call 860-326-1089. Every request is personally reviewed and confirmed.

Is the vehicle cleaned before service?

Yes. The Expedition is washed, detailed, and prepared before every service for a polished and comfortable arrival.
