Privacy & Data Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Who We Are

Empire Elite Rides provides private chauffeur transportation in Albany, New York and the Capital Region. Questions about this policy or your information can be directed to 860-326-1089.

We collect only the information reasonably needed to review transportation requests, communicate with customers, manage reservations, protect the service, and operate this website.

Information You Provide

  • Full name, email address, telephone number, and preferred contact method.
  • Occasion, service date, start and end times, pickup address, destination, trip direction, and notes you choose to provide.
  • Information shared during later telephone, text, or email conversations about a requested service.

Information Collected Automatically

Our hosting, security, font, browser-module, and database providers may process limited technical information such as an IP address, browser type, device information, request timestamps, and error or security logs. This site uses local browser storage to remember your cookie-notice preference.

How We Use Information

  • To check availability, prevent scheduling conflicts, review a request, and confirm or decline service.
  • To contact you by your selected method about the requested ride.
  • To maintain reservation records, administer upcoming services, provide customer support, and resolve disputes.
  • To secure, troubleshoot, maintain, and improve the website and private administration tools.
  • To meet applicable legal, accounting, insurance, safety, or recordkeeping obligations.

Service Providers

We use vendors that support website hosting, fonts, browser modules, database functionality, and internal notifications. Convex processes and stores reservation records and protected administrator sessions. Pushover delivers new-booking notifications to the administrator. Google provides web fonts, and esm.sh delivers the Convex browser module used by the reservation form. These providers may process technical data under their own policies.

Sharing and Sale of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers working on our behalf, when required by law, to protect safety or legal rights, or as part of a business transfer. Reservation details are not displayed publicly.

Retention

Reservation requests and related communications are retained for as long as reasonably necessary for scheduling, customer service, business records, dispute resolution, safety, and legal compliance. Retention periods may vary by record type.

Your Choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of personal information by calling us, subject to information we are legally or operationally required to retain. You may decline non-essential browser storage in the cookie notice or clear local storage through your browser.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, including protected administrator access and server-side validation. No transmission or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure; avoid placing unnecessary sensitive information in the notes field.

Children

The online reservation form is intended for adults arranging transportation. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through this website.

Policy Changes

We may update this policy as our services or legal obligations change. The current version and revision date will remain available on this page.
